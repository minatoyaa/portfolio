Mugiwara Clan

Clash of Clans, developed by Supercell in 2012, is a popular strategy game centered around building villages, training troops, and most importantly, participating in clan wars. Over time, clan wars became the main competitive feature of the game, requiring teamwork, planning, and coordination. To better represent my clan, track wars, and connect with new teammates, I developed a dedicated website powered by the Clash of Clans API .

  • The Problem :

    • Limited in-game tools for managing and tracking wars.
    • Difficulty in showcasing clan progress and achievements.
    • Lack of a central hub to recruit and organize new members.
    • Hard to monitor war status in real time outside the game.

  • Our Solution :

    • Website integrates Clash of Clans API to display real-time data.
    • War progress and achievements are clearly presented online.
    • Dedicated pages to highlight the clan’s identity and attract recruits.
    • Centralized hub that improves organization and communication.
